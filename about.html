About The Artist

A digital illustrator who takes inspiration from Japanese anime and Western comic art. Also dabbles in photography and design when prodded with a stick.

Meozu AKA Zuleika Bostel is a student digital artist based in Wellington New Zealand.

She continuosly explores the grotesque to the beautiful, specialising in the human form and beasts.

Meozu exhibits at local conventions selling prints and merchandise of her art. She also supports her student life through digital art commissions online.

In her spare time she also has a passion for hoop dance, and has won a school talent quest doing just that.
